IEEE

IEEE-RIT has various chapters under it which are working continuously together in bringing up the technical standards of the students of Ramaiah Institute of Technology.

  1. Robo-RIT, the robotics chapter of IEEE-RIT
  2. WIE, Women in Engineering chapter of IEEE-RIT
  3. The Power and Energy Society (PES)
  4. The Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS)
For queries contact - Chandana Keerthi and Rakesh Sangoli

Entrepreneur Development Centre (E Cell)

The Entrepreneur Development Centre(EDC) is an organisation run by the students of M.S.Ramaiah Institute of technology(MSRIT).Ecell focus on building a work culture amongst ourselves and instil confidence and competency to be able to critically analyse our plan and provide constructive feedback that will help in the overall redesign and delivery of training.

National Service Scheme (NSS)

National Service Scheme, popularly known as NSS. The NSS Unit of Ramaiah Institute of Technology was first introduced in the year 1999 and saw a massive support by the students as well as the faculty. NSS MSRIT is a student body organisation run by the students of MSRIT. The ultimate goal of NSS is to imbibe a sense of empathy and responsibility towards the betterment of the country.
